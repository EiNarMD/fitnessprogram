Benefits of Yoga:

Yoga is a holistic practice that benefits the mind and body. It improves flexibility, strength, and balance. Beyond physical benefits, yoga helps in reducing stress and promoting relaxation through breathing exercises and meditation.

About Yoga:

Yoga involves various poses and movements that engage different muscle groups in your body. It emphasizes breathing techniques and mindfulness.

  • Poses for flexibility
  • Strength-building movements
  • Breathing exercises
  • Meditation

Yoga can be practiced anywhere and requires minimal equipment, making it accessible for people of all ages and fitness levels.

How to Practice Yoga:

If you're new to yoga, here are some basics to get started:

  • Start with simple poses and focus on your breathing.
  • Find a quiet, comfortable space to practice.
  • Use a yoga mat or soft surface to prevent slipping.
  • Follow instructional videos or join classes for guidance.
  • Listen to your body and avoid overexertion.
